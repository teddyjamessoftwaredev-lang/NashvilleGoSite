Middle Tennessee Go Club

Welcome to the Middle Tennessee Go Club!


We are a group of Go players from Nashville, Murfreesboro, and the surrounding areas. Players of all levels are welcome, from seasoned players to complete beginners!

People of all ages and walks of life play Go every day around the world. Go is widely popular across Europe and Asia where it is known as Baduk, Igo, Weiqi or Go. It is not until recently that Go has started to spread to the west, but in the United States, people are enjoying Go in record numbers.
